# 2026-07-02

Starry Night Cam v2 — brought Skyline Mode to the perspective camera, then a pass of interaction
affordances (cursor + glyphs + controls guide). Detail in the decision note; this is the arc.

## Perspective Skyline reframe — [[decision-perspective-skyline-reframe]]
- The long-running "low / flat perspective" problem resolved. Root cause named: at a flat aim the
  orbit rig couples `eye.y == target.y`, so you can't drop the camera without dropping the focal.
- Landed split: **LMB** rotates / tilts around a fixed pin (synthesised mid-map pivot when flat),
  drag-up flattens + descends, floor **1 m** (was 5 m); **RMB up/down** pedestals the **coupled**
  eye + focal (altitude, floored 1 m); **RMB left/right** trucks. Tilt clamped `≥ 0`.
- Ortho unchanged — keeps its focal-offset lens-shift reframe (now gated to full ortho).
- Rejected en route: auto-descent (ground pull), GE re-centre, pure lens shift. See the note.

## Interaction affordances
- **Deferred pin**: the orbit pivot pin is captured on press but only shown once the drag crosses a
  ~3 px threshold — a click / double-click-to-zoom no longer flashes it.
- **Cursor state machine**: `pointer` (idle) → `grab` (Shift or Ctrl/⌘ armed) → `grabbing` (only
  once a drag actually moves, not on bare press).
- **Modifier glyphs**: while over the canvas, Shift shows the move icon and Ctrl/⌘ the eye icon,
  riding the cursor — mirrors the mouse-down options and flows straight into the drag.

## Controls guide
- **Look Around** row now shows the LMB+RMB chord glyph, sub "or Ctrl + LMB".
- Right-aligned scene-affordance icons per row: blue pin (Rotate & Tilt), move arrows (Move), eye
  (Look Around) — same sky-blue the scene renders them in.
- Added **Ortho / Perspective (P)** toggle and **R · Reset camera** to the v2 sheet.
