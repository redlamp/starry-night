## Morning — citygen speedups + IndexedDB repeat-visit cache (prebake worktree)

Retroactive log of the `feature/prebake-default-city` worktree (separate session,
now merged).

- **Byte-identical gen speedup ~13%** (`5811e88`): RK4 integrator inlined in the
  tensor street trace + `RoadIndex` switched to integer keys. Golden-verified —
  same seed in, byte-identical city out (the #63 determinism contract held).
- **Tensor field grid-cache ~1.9×** (`c9a58c4`): the field is sampled on a cached
  grid instead of recomputed per query. Gen ~1.65 s → ~1.45 s combined.
- **IndexedDB repeat-visit cache** (`f118b90`): `genVersion.ts` +
  `bundleFingerprint.ts` + `bundleStore.ts`, wired through `useGeneratedCity`.
  Typed-array wire packing for the city bundle; a returning visitor on the same
  seed/version skips the ~1 s generation entirely. Determinism unaffected (the
  cache is keyed on the gen fingerprint).
- **Full pre-bake explored, then dropped**: a 6.3 MB-gzipped flat pre-bake was
  prototyped and rejected (too heavy). Pivoted to RDP + Catmull-Rom **spline
  encoding** of road centerlines (~6–10× compression) — the compact-storage
  direction — but the spline measurement/prototype is **still pending**, so the
  shipped cache is the typed-array IDB bundle above, not the spline form.
- **Turbopack worktree fix** (`b2ff02c`): junctioned-`node_modules` worktrees need
  an explicit turbopack root or dev server resolves modules through the junction.

## Afternoon → evening — drei camera tuning arc (feat/drei-camera-tuning)

The big interaction pass on the drei `<CameraControls>` orbit bridge (Phase 1
landed 06-08 as `6a0fcfd`, behind `?controls=drei`). Research first, then a long
build/iterate loop with the user.

- **Interaction-model research** (`b20a38d`): created [[camera-interaction-models]]
  — Google Maps / RTS / ortho-game / CAD-DCC / drei-default navigation patterns,
  plus an open-decisions log. Assessed "LMB-drag the pin to set Focal Y": the pin
  is parked at the screen-focus %, so it can't *also* follow the cursor —
  positional drag is impossible. Resolution: a **cursor-locked relative scrub**
  (Pointer-Lock precedent).
- **Focal-Y pin-scrub** (`2941ee7`, `2c3f095`): LMB on the focal pin →
  `cursor:none` + pointer-capture + `movementY × distance × K` → Focal Y, re-aim
  from the same camera position so the *view tilts* without moving the rig (can't
  push the camera through the ground). Range ±1000. Below-horizon hit-test fixed
  (the pin icon flips below ground; the hit box mirrors by `focal.y` sign). Slider
  fills from the 0 origin, tinted to match the pin (sky-blue ≥0 / soil-brown <0).
- **Shift+LMB rotate** (`2941ee7`): modifier twin of RMB rotate for trackpad /
  one-button users (Maps convention); a keydown/keyup listener swaps
  `mouseButtons.left` between ROTATE and the custom pan.
- **Label language** (`35dbd12`, `48b338b`, `9bd4429`): renamed the orbit panel to
  **compass / elevation / distance** (azimuth was too technical, "compass" won);
  screen-focus → **Screen Y** with 0% = top, 100% = bottom; Orbit labels
  title-cased.
- **Screen-Y dimension guide** (`27541e5`, `4a708d0`, `66cf701`, `8d42313`,
  `403ebba`): dragging Focal Y / Screen Y flashes the focal pin (and reverts it to
  its prior on/off state on release). A dimension line (top-of-screen → pin) shows
  the Screen-Y %, drawn as a proper dimension line (end caps + arrowheads),
  offset beside the pin with extension caps that span the pin icon so the pin
  doesn't cover the endpoint. Lives in `ScreenYGuide.tsx`, distinct from the plumb
  line, only while adjusting Screen Y.
- **Markers always-mounted** (#80): focal markers (pin / plumbline / ground dot /
  radius ring) stay mounted and toggle visibility, parked on the focal point at
  priority -1 — kills the drei `<Html>` origin-flash on reveal.
- **LMB+RMB free-look**: decided to **keep** it (the chord still earns its place);
  pin-scrub hardened to bail when RMB joins (`ed2cbf5`).

## Evening — orthographic mode + stars-after-reset (feat/drei-camera-tuning)

- **Ortho mode made to feel good** (`7d16624`, `3934504`, `e3f1fb2`): root-caused
  the clipping — faked ortho keeps the real camera's near/far at the real radius,
  so a small radius + large `orthoSize` parks the camera *among* the city. Fix:
  **park the radius** in ortho (`minDistance = orthoMinRadius`, ~1.8× the tier
  half-extent) and hide the Distance slider (it isn't the zoom — `orthoSize` is).
  Added an **8° elevation floor** + an `allowUnderview` gate (`u` key) for the
  deliberate-underview-later idea. Extended the Focal-Y pin-scrub to ortho by
  hit-testing the pin through the parallel frustum instead of `camera.project`.
  **Fog left intentionally unchanged** — FogTicker's distance-anchored brackets
  keep fog pinned for any radius, and ortho-zoom-not-moving-fog is a documented,
  intentional decision (a scale change, not a distance change).
- **Stars no longer vanish after Reset** (`6b406f2`, GitHub #81): `resetCamera` /
  `revertToSaved` reset the intro + star-intro to `progress: 0` ("not yet woken"),
  and the StarField shader gates on `uStarIntroProgress` with no remount to
  replay → black sky (reload remounts → fine, which is why it only showed after a
  reset). Fix: snap the wake animations to `progress: 1` (woken) on reset/revert.
