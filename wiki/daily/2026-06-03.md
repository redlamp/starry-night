## Highway review + topology research (PM)

- **Highway spike reviewed → rejected as a generator model.** Reads as "child's-drawing"
  planning: `generateFreeways` draws primitives (chords through centre + an unanchored
  ring) from `centerX/centerZ/half` with zero knowledge of the city fabric. Beltway
  bypasses nothing; radials connect no destinations; crossings arbitrary. Root cause: the
  macro topology was vibes (junction-scale research was solid; network-topology was the
  unsettled Open Q#1/#2).
- **User design steer:** highways outside the densest blocks (visibility + realism),
  perpendicular crossings w/ interchanges, OK to bulldoze a block (service-scale),
  cloverleaf aesthetic.
- **Topology research pass — closed Open Q#1/#2.** Source: Taillanter & Barthelemy, *Phys.
  Rev. E* 107, 034304 (2023), 888 US cities → freeway emerges ~10k commuters, **ring
  ~100k (≈10× later)**; <1.4M pop = freeway-through-core is net-negative. Cloverleaf
  footprint **30–40 acres ≈ 350–400 m superblock** (r≈175–200 m); stack smaller/taller.
  → **No ring at City scale; skirt the core; ring + system interchanges are Metro-tier.**
  Written to [[highway-network-references]] §6/§7.
- **Disposition:** sequencing decision = *research now (done), build at the #14 scale
  spike*. `feat/highway-crossings` kept as reference/salvage (detection + cloverleaf
  glyph), **not** PR'd. Corridor-model rebuild captured in [[plan-metro-suburbs-highways]].

## Additive-growth architecture decision (PM)

- Ran the additive-growth feasibility pass against the actual gen code. Audit: tensor
  **field** is nearly local (just `N∝scale` + one rng stream); **roads** are global (long
  ~3.4km streamlines, random bbox seeding, order-dependent separation); **districts** are
  global (full-grid flood-fill + distance-to-centre character ranking that re-labels when
  `n` changes — note: districts are no longer Voronoi); **buildings** are per-lot local.
- **Decision → [[decision-additive-growth-citygen]]: generate-at-max + crop** (the user's
  own idea). Always gen field/roads/districts at a fixed MAX (Metro) extent → core is
  extent-invariant, never re-rolls on a slider change; the size slider is a **crop**;
  buildings materialise lazily within the crop. Sidesteps road seam-coherence + the global
  district globals by computing the full layout once and hiding the rest. Distinction:
  *generation* (cheap, at max, invariant) vs *materialisation* (mesh/instances, lazy,
  crop-gated). Also fixes seed-portability. Bounded by MAX extent (fine; Option A
  coordinate-local chunking is the future infinite upgrade).
- **This unblocks the #14 scale spike** (and thus the suburbs + highway-corridor rebuilds).
