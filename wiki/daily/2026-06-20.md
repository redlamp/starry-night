- **`/camera-lab`** ŌĆö the grey-box testbed, now feature-complete for this round (see
  [[camera-lab-test-plan]]): 7 methods, persisted survey, live tuning.
  - **Projection morph** persp<->ortho: started as an element-wise matrix lerp, which
    **snapped at the end** (clip-w stays perspective-dominated until t~=0.999 at city
    depths). Rebuilt as a **receding-eye** morph (camera fixed, virtual eye -> infinity)
    ŌĆö smooth across the whole blend. Toggle via `P`, the sidebar switch, or **tapping
    the diagram's projection label** (the mobile path).
  - **Side-view diagram**: live elevation cross-section (camera, cone-vs-slab frustum,
    elevation arc, focal + plumb, ground, clearance). Fit holds a **constant scale**
    across the morph (always fits the ortho-slab extent). Painted from a ref at display
    rate (was throttled to ~8fps and looked choppy; now ~60).
  - **FPS badge** (current + recent-low), **Focal-Y ground detent** (sticky snap to 0),
    **mobile pin-scrub Focal-Y** (1-finger-on-pin), **ground clamp + ortho horizon
    floor**, **near-horizon pan limit** (kills the grazing "jump to the other side").
  - **Sidebar** refactored to shadcn (Label/Switch/Button) + collapsible sections, to
    match the other labs + main app.
- **THREE.Clock deprecation spam** ŌĆö it's R3F 9.6.1 calling `new THREE.Clock()` per

  `beforeInteractive` console filter in `app/layout.tsx`. Verified suppressed.
