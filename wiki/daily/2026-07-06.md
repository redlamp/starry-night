## Shipped — inspect building focus/selection → [[decision-inspect-focus-selection]]

Iterated the whole focus/selection feel live with Taylor, then merged `feat/city-features` → `fable` → `dev` → `main` (`cf2c0ef`).

- **Focus vs select.** Single click selects (info panel + selection outline). Double-click or the panel's pin button *focuses*: camera glides in, a district-coloured roof pin appears, orbit-lock pins the pivot to the building's 3D centre, and the pin button lights active. The pin button now **toggles** focus on/off (unfocus keeps the selection, just drops pin + lock). Selecting a different building drops the prior focus.
- **Shortest-route focus.** `moveTo` + `dollyTo` preserve the current view angle (zero rotation) instead of `setLookAt`, which revolved the long way once the drag-accumulated azimuth unwound.
- **Fit + ortho zoom.** Distance fits the building's bounding sphere to the narrower live FOV. In ortho (distance doesn't scale apparent size) the zoom eases `orthoSize` to ~half the viewport height, on a slow-start smoothstep ramp on the *same useFrame clock* as the pan — position zeroes in first, then the zoom trails (a free-running rAF had read as jerky/desynced).
- **District colour everywhere.** The inverted-hull highlight, the x-ray wireframe (drawn through occluders), the roof pin, and the district outline all take the building's district colour; the district name spans its own full-width panel row.

## Picking fixes (both latent, surfaced by heavy inspect use)

- **Buildings unpickable after the camera moved.** `InstancedMesh.raycast` early-outs on a `boundingSphere` it computes once over the then-visible instances; #55 per-frame tile compaction left it stale, silently dropping picks. Fixed by pinning the sphere over the full instance set at build (buildings never move).
- **Ortho hover/selection mis-picked off-centre.** The faked-ortho camera stays a `PerspectiveCamera`, so R3F's raycaster built diverging perspective rays. `OrthoPickingFix` builds parallel rays when the live projection matrix reads orthographic.
