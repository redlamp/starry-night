### Inspect building focus/selection → [[decision-inspect-focus-selection]]

Iterated the whole focus/selection feel live, then merged `feat/city-features` → `fable` → `dev` → `main` (`cf2c0ef`).

- **Focus vs select.** Single click selects (info panel + selection outline). Double-click or the panel's pin button *focuses*: camera glides in, a district-coloured roof pin appears, orbit-lock pins the pivot to the building's 3D centre, and the pin button lights active. The pin button **toggles** focus on/off (unfocus keeps the selection). Selecting a different building drops the prior focus.
- **Shortest-route focus.** `moveTo` + `dollyTo` preserve the current view angle (zero rotation) instead of `setLookAt`, which revolved the long way once the drag-accumulated azimuth unwound.
- **Fit + ortho zoom.** Distance fits the bounding sphere to the narrower live FOV. In ortho the zoom eases `orthoSize` to ~half the viewport height on a slow-start smoothstep ramp on the *same useFrame clock* as the pan — position zeroes in first, then the zoom trails (a free-running rAF had read as jerky).
- **District colour everywhere.** Inverted-hull highlight, x-ray wireframe (through occluders), roof pin, and district outline all take the building's district colour; the district name spans its own panel row.
