- **#65 skybox** — stars-vs-city sweep is acceptable (perspective fine, ortho ok). The real issue

  while the stars render in the StarPass (star camera at origin, quaternion-copied). Fix (greenlit):
  render the moon **in the star pass** so it shares the star camera and sweeps in lockstep with the
  stars. See [[moon-rendering]] §"Moon ↔ star render-pass alignment".

- **#54 fog** — user repro: at **1693** from an OFF-CENTRE skyscraper cluster, the buildings are
  fully fogged. Root cause: `FogTicker` keys fog on camera→**CITY_CENTRE** distance, not
  camera→focal/target — so when the camera sits near centre but looks at a far off-centre cluster,
  fog calibrated to the (small) centre distance fogs the cluster out. Fixing the focal-distance
  bug first; broader extent-adaptation plan: [[plan-fog-extent-adaptation]].

## Traffic density targets (#78, at density 1, fully-busy segment)
| Tier | carsPerM × lanes | ≈ cars/km |
|---|---|---|
| Highway | 0.02 × 3 | ~60 (dense ribbon) |
| Arterial | 0.012 × 2 | ~24 |
| City street | 0.008 × 1 | ~8 (≈1 car / 125 m) |
| Suburban street | ×0.12–0.4 busyness | ~1–3 (trickle) |

Caveat: `MAX_CARS = 5000` + the new proportional scaling thins all tiers *together* at large city
tiers, so on-screen densities fall below these uncapped targets for big cities — the viz should show
the **expected (pre-cap)** per-segment density so the intended distribution is legible.
