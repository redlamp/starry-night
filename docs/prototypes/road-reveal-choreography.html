Scout Glint

The guiding light. A point races the highway spine before anything exists — every later act follows the path it announces.

Blueprint Trace

The plan sketching itself behind the light: dim lines draw on, ordered outward from the centre, while the worker generates.

Cascade

The build. Highways grow from their centre-nearest point, arterials sprout at junctions after a beat (LAG), streets snap in behind the wavefront.

Construction Tip

The white band hugging each wavefront. Retires by 92% of the cascade so the closing stragglers land quietly.

Straggler Tail

The late, sparse end of the schedule — far radial orphans. TAIL_EASE bunches them into a closing group instead of lone streaks.

Intro Gate

The hold on the building wake-up until the wave is ~35% out. Roads lead, buildings follow; streetlights and traffic ride the intro timeline.

Settled City

Flat tier colours, tips retired, trace gone. The screensaver proper.
